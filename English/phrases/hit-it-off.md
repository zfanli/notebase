# hit it off

投缘；合得来。

例句：

- They really hit it off. 他们真的很合得来。
- It seems like you two are hitting it off! 你们俩似乎很投缘！

## hit it off with sb.

与某人投缘、合得来。

例句：

- I think you'd really hit it off with her. 我觉得你真的和她很合拍。
- I didn't really hit it off with his friends. 我和他朋友实在是不投缘。
